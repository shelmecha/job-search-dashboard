Every refresh pulls live, client-side, from the Remotive, RemoteOK, and Jobicy public job APIs, the career pages of 31 remote-friendly companies (GitLab, Stripe, Notion, Dropbox, Affirm, Coinbase, Cloudflare, Lattice, Gusto, Webflow, ClickUp, Canonical, Anthropic, Automattic, Zapier, Deel, and more — via their public Greenhouse/Ashby board APIs, filtered to remote-open postings only), and open freelance projects from the Freelancer.com public API. Everything is scored against each role's keyword profile, with contract and worldwide-open roles boosted. Nothing is sent to a server and nothing is stored. LinkedIn, Indeed, Google, and Upwork don't expose public search APIs (and this tool doesn't scrape), so each card links into a pre-filled search on the big boards as a one-click fallback.
